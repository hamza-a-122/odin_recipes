Burger

    Ingredients

  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef (or chicken/turkey)
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon black pepper
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder (optional)
  • 4 burger buns
  • 4 slices of cheese (cheddar or your choice)
  • Lettuce, tomato, onion (optional toppings)
  • Ketchup, mayo, mustard (optional sauces)

    How to prepare a burger

  1. Prepare the patties – In a bowl, mix ground beef with salt, pepper, and garlic powder. Shape into 4 even patties.
  2. Cook the patties – Heat a pan or grill over medium-high heat. Cook patties for about 3-4 minutes per side (or until fully cooked). Add cheese on top during the last minute.
  3. Toast the buns – Lightly toast burger buns on the pan or grill for 1-2 minutes until golden.
  4. Assemble the burger – Place the patty on the bottom bun, add lettuce, tomato, onion, and your favorite sauces.
  5. Serve and enjoy – Place the top bun, press lightly, and serve your homemade burger with fries or salad! 🍔😋
